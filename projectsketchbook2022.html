Digital Sketchbook 2022

My desire to improve my digital skills and abilities prompted me to create a digital sketchbook, where whenever I could, ideally weekly, I would do at least a couple of sketches.

This is the compilation of some of them, some days wanting to practice anatomy, or values, even painting, other days practicing the flow of my lines, and finally other days doing more "finished" illustrations.
