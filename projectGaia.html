Chroma Corps - Character Design

This work was done to enter the Chroma corps Challenge organized by the illustrator Sinix, where we had to design a character who was a school cadet in the context of futurism, sci-fi style.

In general ways, I was inspired by the colors of a chameleon, I wanted at least one very saturated color that would be the main one for the character, and at the same time contrast with the dark tones of the rest of her costume, to better introduce her to the universe of the characters. Chroma corps decided to make him weaponry and mechanical prosthetics to better fit the required concept.
