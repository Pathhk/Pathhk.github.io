Running from Her

This project was largely inspired by the art and style of the LD&R series, the objective of this project was to demonstrate through a sequence guided by a color script the storytelling through the illustrations, for that a work of several thumbnails and color swatches, colors that were crucial in defining parameters and emotions throughout the project.

For this, I also designed this character, who is the protagonist of this story and finds herself fleeing from an unknown entity through the streets, ending up in a subway car where the face of her nightmares finally shows, who turns out to be herself cyborg version, this sequence of illustrations was made to participate in Proko's May contest, where the main theme was storytelling.
