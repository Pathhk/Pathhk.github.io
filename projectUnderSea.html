Illustration - Under the Sea

This project had as its beginning the intention to participate in the 2021 Fnac illustration contest, being so, I decided to take as the initial theme the concept "Under the sea" celebrating the arrival of summer, bringing the concept of how the sea is under our feet it's alive and it's a completely magical world with a lot to explore.

For them, my three illustrations sought to have something in common, the ocean as the initial focus and the sea creatures as the protagonists of the drawings, such as goldfish, white whales, all of them swim in harmony with humans.
