Rift Gala - Skin Character Design

This Skin design was made in collaboration with the Rift Gala, where the concept of magic and fantasy are combined to carry out the version of the MET GALA on behalf of the League of Legends community.

Being so, I chose the character of Zyra and I wanted to give her royal vibes, that's why I chose the purple and gold colors, also her clothing was very inspired by the classic era in combination with the spines and features of the original character.
