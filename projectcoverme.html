Cover me - Project

This project was inspired by the Bastille song "Doom Days" where we can see how a society in decline has to continue and face life even though the earth is falling apart, from there we can see our protagonist, a man middle-aged man who, despite the fateful situation in the world, goes to work as usual and ends up being his last day of work.

This project was carried out as the first project objective of the Design Project II class, the objective was to create a 30-second video that explained/summarized or identified a song chosen by us, to be used, for example, on Spotify.
